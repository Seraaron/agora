### Clothier ([[Endurance|End]])

#laborious #craft #artistic 

Working fine material like wool, cotton, hemp, or silk, into yarn and string, by thrashing the fibers, or carding and felting the wool, and then running them through a spindle or wheel to make threads. Then knitting, or using a loom, to work those materials into bolts of cloth, quilts, rugs, or clothing.

Also includes weaving to make baskets, bags, mats, fencing, or furniture, and making nets or rope, or mending clothes too, using [[Finesse]].
